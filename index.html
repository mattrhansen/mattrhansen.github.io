All About Huck

Huck hails from Marysville, Washington (about half an hour north of Seattle) and has been trailblazing with his parents since 2014. He has a wealth of experience in all types of terrain, especially low alpine environments, but is also skilled in navigating the coast, or trekking across snow if needs be. He is particularly adept at running ahead of the group trying to scout out the nearest competition (possibly a bear). He will help facilitate a smooth hike and help you find the perfect campsite based on his half decade plus of experience. Huck is licensed in Washington and Illinois.
